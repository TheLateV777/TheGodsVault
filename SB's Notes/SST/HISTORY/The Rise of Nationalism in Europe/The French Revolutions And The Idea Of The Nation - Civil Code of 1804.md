The French Revolutions And The Idea Of The Nation
- Nationalism came with French revolution in 1789
- The nation's political and constitutional changes led to transfer of sovereignty from Monarchy to Citizens.
- from the beginning, revolutionaries introduced measures and practices that could create a sense of identity.
- The ideas la patrie (father land) and la citoyen (the citizen) showed unity in all communities enjoying rights of the constitution.
- A new tri colour flag introduced to replace the royal one.
- Uniform systems of weights and measures were adopted.
Civil Code of 1804

- did away with all privilages based on birth

## Asymmetrical Federalism in India

- Federations formed by **“holding together”** do not distribute power equally among all constituent units.
- In the **Indian Union**, states do not possess identical powers.
- Some states enjoy **special constitutional status** due to unique social and historical circumstances. And few with less privileges like Union Territories
- There are states which enjoy privileges Known as **States with special provision (Article 371)**..
- And there is states which enjoy less privileges than others known as **The Union Territories**..

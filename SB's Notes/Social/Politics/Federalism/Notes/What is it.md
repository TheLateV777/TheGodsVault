federalism is a system of government in which power is divided between a central authority and various constituent units of the country.

A federation has two lvl of government.
one is the government for the entire country that is usually responsible for few subjects of common national interest.
The other governments at the lvl of provinces or states that  of provinces or states that look after much of the day to day administering of their state.
*mostly these two lvl of government enjoy their power independent of the other*
The central govt  cannot order the the state govt to do smtng. State govt has powers of its own for which it is not answerable to the central govt.
